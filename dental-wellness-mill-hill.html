About Dental Wellness Mill Hill

Dental Wellness Mill Hill is a private dental practice on The Broadway in Mill Hill, NW7. Check-ups start at £45 and Invisalign from £3,695. Open 7 days a week including until 8pm on Mondays — one of the most accessible practices in North West London. Rated 5.0 stars from over 555 reviews, with 0% finance available.

The practice serves patients across Mill Hill, Edgware, Hendon, Finchley and surrounding NW7.
