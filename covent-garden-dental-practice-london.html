About Covent Garden Dental Practice

Covent Garden Dental Practice is on Odhams Walk in WC2, a short walk from Covent Garden tube (Piccadilly line). Check-ups start at £119 and the practice offers hygiene, cosmetic dentistry and Invisalign. Open Monday to Saturday. Rated 4.8 stars from 145 reviews.

The practice serves patients across Covent Garden, Holborn, Soho and surrounding WC2.
