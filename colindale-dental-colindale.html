About Colindale Dental

Colindale Dental is a mixed NHS and private dental practice located on Colindale Avenue in NW9, North West London. With 629 Google reviews and a 4.7-star rating, it is one of the most-reviewed and highly regarded dental practices in the Colindale and Burnt Oak area.

The practice offers both NHS and private dental treatments, including routine check-ups, hygiene appointments, white fillings, crowns, root canal treatment, teeth whitening, and Invisalign clear aligners. The practice is open Monday to Saturday, including early morning appointments on weekdays.

Colindale Dental serves patients across Colindale, Burnt Oak, Hendon, Edgware, Kingsbury and the wider NW9 area. Its location on Colindale Avenue makes it easily accessible from the Colindale tube station and surrounding residential areas.
