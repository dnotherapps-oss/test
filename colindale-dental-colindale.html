About Colindale Dental

Colindale Dental is a well-regarded dental practice offering both private and NHS services at 49-51 Colindale Avenue, NW9. Check-ups start at £55 and Invisalign from £4,000. Open Monday to Saturday with extended Wednesday and Thursday hours. Rated 4.7 stars from over 629 reviews.

The practice serves patients across Mill Hill, Edgware, Hendon, Finchley and surrounding NW7.
