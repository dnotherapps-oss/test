About Care Dental Smile

Care Dental Smile on Stag Lane in Burnt Oak, Edgware, HA8, accepts NHS and private patients. Check-ups are £40 and composite fillings start at £80. Open Monday to Saturday. Rated 4.8 stars from 253 reviews.

The practice serves patients across Mill Hill, Edgware, Hendon, Finchley and surrounding NW7.
