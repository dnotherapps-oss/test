About Glow Dentistry

Glow Dentistry is a private dental practice on Ballards Lane in North Finchley, N3, offering general dentistry, cosmetic treatments, Invisalign and dental implants. Check-ups start at £95 and Invisalign from £1,500. Close to West Finchley tube (Northern line). Rated 4.9 stars from 74 reviews.

The practice serves patients across North Finchley, Finchley, Barnet and surrounding N3.
